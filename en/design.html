design Thiking

The project to be presented by Design Thinking is the following: Observing and knowing the work that a painter performs we realize one of the most frequent problems that face when painting worth the redundadncia. At the moment of working with the painter, he not only faces physical problems but also comes to the moment of carrying out his work. The problems that can be perceived in general is that when you finish painting you have a muscular fatigue because of the position you are in at the moment of performing this action and the lack of security that you have when climbing a structure that It can be dangerous and fatal stories like the chair, benches, stairs, among others. The questions that can be obtained are: when you want to paint but you can not reach very high places, like in a wall or on a wall, you need structures, that the apparatus with which you paint is very short, and the time demand what it takes to carry out this work. Being able to give a tentative solution to the problem would be to be able to create and facilitate the painter a tool that is adapted to the work to avoid accidents and that can reduce the time of execution in the work.
